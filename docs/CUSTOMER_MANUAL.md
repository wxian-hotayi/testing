# Customer Manual

## Shopping
- Browse **Shop** or category pages. Each product page shows ingredients,
  benefits, usage, reviews, and bundle pricing.
- **Bundle & save**: pick 1, 2, or 3 bottles — savings are shown live.
- **Subscribe & save 15%**: toggle on a product page and choose monthly or
  quarterly delivery.

## Cart & checkout
- Adding an item opens the **cart drawer** with a free-shipping progress bar
  (free over RM 200), suggested add-ons, and a discount-code field.
- Click **Checkout** to pay securely via Stripe. You'll enter shipping and card
  details on Stripe's hosted page. A confirmation email follows.

## Your account
Sign in (email or Google) to access:
- **Dashboard**: points, active subscriptions, recent orders.
- **Orders**: full history + tracking links.
- **Subscriptions**: pause, skip the next order, change address, or cancel —
  anytime, no fees.
- **Rewards**: earn 1 point per RM 1 spent; redeem points for discount codes.
- **Referrals**: share your link — when a friend's first order ships, you both
  earn rewards.
- **Addresses, Wishlist, Profile**: manage saved addresses, saved products, and
  your details / marketing preferences.

## Discounts
Enter codes in the cart or checkout. New customers get **WELCOME10** (10% off
the first order).

## Help
Questions? See the **FAQ** or email support (see the Contact page). Our products
are supplements, not medicines — consult a healthcare professional before use if
you are pregnant, nursing, on medication, or have a medical condition.
